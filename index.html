🐾 How to play 🐱
  1. Tap a Kanji brick to start a word.
  2. Then find and tap its readings (and meaning) — each glides into the review tray:
    • Kanji — the character itself
    • On-yomi — the Chinese-derived reading (usually カタカナ)
    • Kun-yomi — the native Japanese reading (ひらがな)
    • Meaning — English or Español (hidden in 日本語 mode)
  3. Complete the set and the word floats away with a little jingle. ✨

🎚️ Easy guides you step-by-step through matching each kanji; Hard removes all guidance (no colors, no gray-out help, on-yomi shown in hiragana).

⚙️ Settings (top-right) — change difficulty, kanji count, language & sound.

📈 Progress — how well you know each kanji (more ★ = stronger).

🍡 Collection — words you’ve cleared: see each kanji used in a real sentence, plus its history, related kanji, and where you’ll spot it.
